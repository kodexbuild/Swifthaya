About this role

About this role As a UI/UX Designer at Kodex , you will focus on design user-friendly on serval platform (web, mobile , dashboards etc) to our users need. Your innovative solution will enhance the user experience on several platforms. Join us and let’s make impact on user friendly designs/interaction at Kodex Africa.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design engaging and responsive user interfaces for web and mobile applications.
  • Collaborate with UX designers to ensure seamless user experiences.
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs.
  • Work with developers to ensure accurate implementation of designs.
  • Maintain brand consistency across all design elements.
  • Conduct user research and usability testing to improve designs.

Requirements:

  • Proven experience as a UI Designer or in a similar role.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, or Photoshop.
  • Strong portfolio showcasing UI design projects.
  • Understanding of design principles, typography, and color theory.
  • Familiarity with front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) is a plus.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in designing for mobile-first applications.
  • Knowledge of accessibility standards and design best practices.
  • Experience working in an Agile development environment.
  • At least 2-4 years of relevant experience in product design or related roles
  • Have good knowledge using figma and figjam
